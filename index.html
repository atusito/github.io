About Lakewood

Lakewood is a paranormal and mystery story about a group of misfit young adults and the town they grew up in. They all experienced a strange event in their childhoods that has linked them together. Curiously, most of them seem to barely remember what had happened, except that one of their friends went missing for a while.

Now in their 20s, they're trying to start lives for themselves and get out of their dead end town - except it's proving a challenge.

Recently, however, strange things have started to happen again in Lakewood - including another disappearance.

The project was originally planned as a novel series, however someday we'd love to create a comic if able. For now tho, it's just another DVSKFALL project on the back burner.

Roleplay for this project is restricted to friends only, and would have to take place in an AU as this project has a set storyline.

The Setting

Lakewood

Lakewood is a small vacation town nestled up in the Catskill mountains in upsate NY. The town has a small local population, but tourism and vacationing have dried up considerably since the 90s. Still, the town manages to get by. As beautiful as the lakeside town is, it's a dead end for anyone with ambition. Many of the residence can't seem to successfully move on or plant there roots elsewhere, despite their desire.

Lake Clearwater

While Lake Clearwater, the town's namesake, was a bustling vacation spot mid-century up through the 80s, it has been in decline since. The locals of course blame the economy, though there is something peculiar about the Lake that's hard to deny. An incident in the mid 90s where some local kids went missing for a while seems to have tainted the vacation spot's reputation. Now only locals seem to visit the lakeside with any regularity. Curiously, what visitors they do get seem to always cut their stay short.

The Mystery Vortex

At the Northern edge of Lakewood is the infamous 'Mystery Vortex' which locals are divided on being either their town's only standing place of pride, or just another embarassment. This Mystery spot was a bustling tourist attraction for roadtrippers on the interstate up through the mid 90s, even when the appeal of Lake Clearwater ran dry. Being in decline through the 2000s, it was recently purchased and fixed up by an ambitious young local, who has successfully turned it back into a highway destination with the help of viral internet marketing and a rather sketchy youtube channel.

Lakeside Bar & Grill

One of the more popular night life destinations in Lakewood, which isn't saying much. Located just off the interstate at the eastern most edge of Lake Clearwater, it happens to be a place popular both with locals and with travelers. They're known in the area for their incredible burgers and fries and cozy atmosphere (though also unfortunately infamous for their local drunks as well). It's also a great place to pick up the local gossip.
